I am a media researcher with Dublin City University’s Institute for Future Media, Democracy and Society looking at how digital disruption impacts journalism practice.

My focus is on the deployment of technological innovation within newsrooms, such as the use of algorithmic solutions to generate news stories.

Before joining Dublin City University on a Marie Skłodowska-Curie fellowship, I worked as a multiplatform journalist and digital news producer for Canada’s public service broadcaster, CBC/Radio-Canada, where I oversaw a team of reporters and engaged with new formats.
