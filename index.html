I am a media researcher with Dublin City University’s Institute for Future Media and Journalism looking at how digital disruption transforms journalism practices.

My focus is on the deployment of AI solutions within newsrooms, such as the use of automated journalism to generate news stories or the development of innovative products to debunk fake news.

Before joining Dublin City University on a Marie Skłodowska-Curie fellowship, I worked as a multiplatform journalist and digital news producer for Canada’s public service broadcaster, CBC/Radio-Canada, where I oversaw a team of reporters and engaged with new formats.
