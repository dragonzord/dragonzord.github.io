Samuel Danzon-Chambaud is an Early Stage Researcher at Dublin City University’s School of Communications and Institute for Future Media and Journalism (FuJo).

His research focuses on the rising strategies involving automated journalism within newsrooms. He holds a Master of Journalism from the University of British Columbia in Vancouver and an M.A. in History from Pantheon-Sorbonne University in Paris.

Before joining FuJo on a Marie Skłodowska-Curie fellowship, he worked for Canada’s public service broadcaster, CBC/Radio-Canada, where he specialised in online news and new formats.
