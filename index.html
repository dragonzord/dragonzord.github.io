I am a researcher at the design consultancy frog.co, where I study algorithmic practice through the lens of participatory design.

Before joining frog.co, I was a PhD researcher with Dublin City University’s Institute for Future Media, Democracy and Society. My thesis was on the use of algorithmic solutions to generate news stories.

I also worked at Canada’s public service broadcaster, CBC/Radio-Canada, where I oversaw a team of reporters and engaged with new formats.
